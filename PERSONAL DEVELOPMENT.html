PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

Accountability, humility, discipline I can see a number of ways in which these characteristics are currently influencing my life.
For starters, I'm not one to brag about my successes or what I have in on social media, and I rarely share personal information unless it's specifically requested, so humility is a virtue of mine. My family members and friends, to be honest, are unaware of my future objectives and some achievements in life since I believe it is needless to discuss them and for society to know, and I prefer to keep my ambitionsprivate and personal to me. Many people ask me for life counsel, which I find strange because I do not consider myself to be the wisest person. Even my mother has come to me to chat about her problems at work, and she has always agreed with the counsel I have given her, despite the fact that I appear mediocre at offering advice.

Secondly accountability is important in my current life since I am an individual performer who expects to be held accountable for my results, therefore I aim to do everything perfectly. I try to be a trustworthy person who is known for correctness and precision in completing projects, whether they are homework assignments, laboratories, or just everyday events, throughout my schoolwork and interactions with friends. I also like having more control and responsibility, therefore I frequently accept extra responsibilities, especially when working in groups, without being particularly thrilled because it gives me more leadership and accountability. My future objective is aided by the knowledge that I am strong in responsibilities.

My third strength is discipline, which is useful in my current situation because I am constantly writing lists, keeping an agenda, and attempting to plan for all of the duties that I am taking on. Also, when it comes to academics, I am happy for deadlines since they help me organize my daily duties. I also utilize discipline to make things more efficient by employing daily routines to help me complete projects with attention to detail. Using my discipline, I tend to squander less time, allowing me to devote more time to the people, events, and organizations that I find fascinating and that require my attention. Discipline helps me achieve one of my future goals of remaining organized and efficient.
